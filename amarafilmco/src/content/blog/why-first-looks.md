A first look is a private moment before the ceremony. It can settle nerves, free up your cocktail hour, and give us soft, honest light to work with.

If tradition matters more to you than logistics, skip it. We have photographed stunning days both ways. What we care about is that the choice feels true to you—not borrowed from a checklist.

Talk with us about light, location, and family expectations. We will help you choose a plan that still leaves room for surprise during your vows.
