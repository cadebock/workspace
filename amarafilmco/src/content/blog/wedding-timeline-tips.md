Start from sunset (or your ceremony time) and work backward. Buffer transitions: getting ready runs long, family photos take focus, and you will want a breath between events.

Build in **fifteen minutes of nothing** after your first look. That quiet becomes some of our favorite frames—and it keeps you from feeling rushed into vows.

Share your must-haves with your planner and us early. When priorities are clear, we can protect them without turning your day into a spreadsheet.
